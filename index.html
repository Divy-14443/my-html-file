India is a country with many different cultures and religions. It is the only country in the world that refuses to accept the slogan "Unity in Diversity."
In comparison to the rest of the world, India has more festivals. Some of these festivals celebrate the arrival of the seasons, harvest, rains, and even the full moon, while others celebrate religious events such as Gods' birthdays or the start of the local New Year.
The majority of Indian festivals are observed throughout the country, but some are known by different names in different parts of the country.
